Role Access At A Glance
Fraud Analyst

Handle manual reviews and appeals

Analysts own the active decision queue and are the recorded actor for claim, release, and final outcome actions.

Fraud review console
  • Claim flagged cases
  • Approve or reject reviews
  • Claim and resolve appeals
Fraud Manager

Oversee fraud operations and outcomes

Managers keep access to the review lane while also monitoring the aggregate fraud and appeals picture.

Fraud review console Manager dashboard
  • Review active operations
  • Access analytics and audit views
  • Track team outcomes and trends
Ops Readonly

Inspect platform health and telemetry

Readonly ops access is intentionally separate from fraud handling so observability stays available without queue privileges.

Manager dashboard Grafana Jaeger Prometheus cAdvisor
  • Read-only observability access
  • Performance and tracing visibility
  • No fraud-case handling rights
Ops Admin

Operate the telemetry and local email stack

Ops admins can open the same observability tools plus Mailpit for local SMTP and OTP message inspection.

Manager dashboard Grafana Jaeger Prometheus cAdvisor Mailpit
  • Everything ops-readonly can access
  • Mail inbox inspection for local SMTP
  • No fraud-case handling rights
Sign In

Open only what your role is allowed to see

The gateway issues the staff session, and the protected surfaces use it to gate the UI and observability entry points.

Redirects stay role-safe If you open a protected page before signing in, the system will only send you back there if your role is actually permitted to access it.
Customer banking stays separate
Customers do not use these staff accounts. They continue through the banking portal.
